Overview

✦

What is
TRON: ARES?

" A hybrid action RPG and strategic territory-control game where each mission changes the balance of power. Design your Program, hack sectors, and move from skirmishes to large-scale Grid domination. "

Set deep within the digital frontier of the Grid, TRON: ARES fuses lightning-fast disc combat with the slow-burn tension of territorial conquest. Every sector won, every program upgraded, every enemy derezz'd reshapes the landscape for the next mission.

Whether you're a lone operative cutting through enemy lines or a faction commander coordinating Grid-wide campaigns, the world responds to your every move. No two runs through the Grid are ever the same.

✦ ✦ ✦
  • I

    Faction Campaign

    Push influence across the Grid and shape how regions answer to your faction. Alliances shift — loyalty must be earned through action.

  • II

    Program Designer

    Build specialized loadouts combining mobility, defense, and disc mastery. Every Program tells a different story on the battlefield.

  • III

    Grid Infiltration

    Hack routes, disable defenses, and use the environment as part of the fight. The Grid is your weapon as much as your arena.
